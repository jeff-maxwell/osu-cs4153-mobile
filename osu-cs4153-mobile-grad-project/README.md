# CS4153 Mobile App Development

## Graduate Project – 50 Points
#### Last updated: Oct 25, 2018 2:30 PM 
#### Due: Dec 7 11:59 PM NO LATE SUBMISSIONS!

This assignment is required only for students who are enrolled in the course for graduate credit. It is an app that involves Core ML 2.

1. Write a Swift 4.2 app using Xcode 10 for iOS 12 that uses Core ML 2 to learn to
distinguish the condition of railroad boxcars based on the amount of rust, corrosion,
and such observed in color photos of boxcars.
- There are many sources of such photos on the web.

2. The app that you turn in should already be trained (no pun intended). When it is run,
the user should be able to present the app with a color boxcar photo it has not seen, and
the app should make a reasonable estimate of the boxcar’s condition.
- Come up with your own scale that includes at least five levels of condition.

3. Write a user’s guide for your app. It must be in PDF form when submitted.

4. Write a brief (<= 10 typed pages) report that explains how Core ML 2 works to learn to
solve such problems. It must be in PDF form when submitted.

Grading Notes
- It is very important that you appropriately reference your sources, whether they be
sources for parts of your app, your user’s guide, or your report.
- If you copy materials into your project without appropriately referencing them, you
could get a grade of zero for the project.
